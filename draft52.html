    Instructions:
  1. indicate the number of notes in your melody by clicking the 'last' button which corresponds with the last note
  2. select your notes: select an opening key from any of the three adjustable cells in the first column and melody notes in the melody cells of each remaining column
  3. calibrate a harmony by clicking 'left to right'
  4. if your resting note was not already selected, select it in the last column
  5. click 'right to left' to calibrate the harmony of your closing key with the harmony of your opening key
