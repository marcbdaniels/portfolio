GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Every Customer is different. Every Client is our Customer.

We know that your business is unique; so is your company's culture. Engentive puts you in charge of everything that makes you different. From marketplace customizations and design to product selection, pricing, marketing, and much more. We don't have a "one-size fits all" approach, because "one size" rarely fits perfectly.

Customer satisfaction is not a privilege - it's a choice.

Too often organizations are not doing what they need to do with their operations and corporate training to make customer service a core competency. They disregard great customer service as a privilege only delivered when it's convenient to them, or required of them to stay in business. We believe that amazing customer service is not a privilege; it's a choice. It's a choice we make to "wow" our customers with every single thing we do. It's also a choice our customers and respected clients make when deciding to do business with us. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service with every action we take. In fact, everything we do begins and ends with our obsession to deliver happieness.

Committed to growth and sustainability.

...Not only our growth, but yours too! We have solemnly committed to running our business honestly, transparently, and with a clear focus on providing a growing and thriving work environment. This means we care about things like company culture, core values, and sustainability practices. We also give back to our community and volunteer our efforts to international charitable organizations.

Engentive is "people-powered"

Developed from the ground up to provide solutions which serve and empower and entire industry, Engentive is part of KLF Media inc.

We've been into Premium and Incentive for over 10 years now. We've expertly gathered all of our accumulated industry knowledge and married this information to today's best practices in eCommerce to create Engentive.

We believe it's our competitive advantage.
