Technology

Engentive is powered by a set proprietary algorythms and processes which work in unison to power your rewards marketplace. We call this the Rewards Enhanced Validation (REVx) Engine. It's the motor that keeps your program running smoothly.

Engentive's enterprise-level platform

From 50 to 500,000 members, Engentive's enterprise-level platform can handle your program's traffic no matter the size of your business.

Rewards Manager Application (RMA)

Easily manage your rewards program using Engentive's Rewards Manager Application (RMA). Fully-featured and designed to meet the needs of today's modern enterprise, Rewards Manager allows you to expertly and easily manage your program's marketplace. You decide on which categories, brands, and products you wish to carry. What's more, you'll also be in charge of points conversion, on-page marketing, and most every other important aspect of your customized marketplace.

Customer-Facing Application (CFA)

Engentive's Customer-Facing Application (CFA) acts as your rewards marketplace storefront. This is where your program members do their shopping. Much like an eCommerce store, the Engentive CFA makes it super simple to find, add, and purchase items you've made available to your program participants. You decide all aspects of what your members see and when they see it. Control individual permissions and access rights, points attribution, and more. Engentive's CFA is where your program comes to life!

FULFILLMENT ENGINE

When your member places an order, it is automatically routed to our nearest warehouse for immediate expedition. Your member receives an automated ship notification with tracking within a few hours of the gift being shipped.

PROGRAM TYPES

Whether it be for employee retention, sales force motivation, service recognition, contest & giveaways or customer-based loyalty programs, Engentive's flexible platform architecture supports your rewards strategy in any form it takes.

DATA ANALYTICS MODULE

Stay informed and in control every step of the way with full data reporting. View order history, redemption patterns, points balance or any other information in real time on your personalised dashboard.

CUSTOMIZATION

A user-friendly customization wizard provides you full control over color, images, wording, communications and even the domain name (URL) you wish to use for your customized marketplace. Settings can be changed anytime within your client dashboard.

CATALOGUE MANAGEMENT

Engentive sources and updates thousands of products and brands for you. Using simple filtering tools in your account, you can narrow down the rewards you wish to offer by brand, category, pricing or other criteria.

INTEGRATION TYPES

APIs

A REST based API allows real-time data exchange between your member database and your reward marketplace. This integration is the most robust and ensures data accuracy at all times.

XML

XML feeds dropped on your sFTP allow you to dictate when you want data updates made to your member point balance. Once parsed by Engentive, XML updates provide a reliable way to exchange data.

CSV

You want to keep things simple? No problem! We can exchange your member infromation and point updates using CSV or XLS files sent via email, or dropped on your sFTP. Engentive has safeguards in place to ensure data integrity is respected.
